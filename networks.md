## Look at the network we are on
* Gives you IP addresses from URLs (DNS)
* Gives you the time (should not be an issue)
* Phone network, Home, Guest (coffee shop), work (will have a proxy)

## VPNs
* Will
    - hide network activity from ISP or provider
    - can provide another location for traffic
* Will NOT
    - hide browsing from the VPN provider
    - hide accounts or IDs
    - hide cookies or history
* How do they make money?

## Proxies
* A trusted proxy can decrypt your SSL connections
* Most companies will have to have proxies at this point and they WILL look at your internet traffic
* Assume that anyplace that provides you a system, is monitoring traffic
* A pop-up that asks you to trust a certificate provider may also be doing this
