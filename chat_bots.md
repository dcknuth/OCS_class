## Use Agreements
* Can the company use anything you type in for any purpose
* How do they make money?
    - Many of these will or do use an ad based revenue
    - Businesses pay to keep their information proprietary
* How do they get more training data?

## Context window
This is how a system can remember what you were talking about and personalize to your taste. Also would be good to sell for ads

## Related access
Be cautious about giving any system access as you
* Email
* Cloud drives
* Local access
* Device access
* Account access

## Scam Time: The Nigerian Prince
The original went something like this... I am the heir to the throne in Nigeria. I have only recently discovered this after living in the U.S. for many years. Due to several cancer deaths and an assassination, the line has unexpectedly reached me. I will have a stipend of almost a million dollars a year, but I have to get to Nigeria. If you can get me $5,000 for a plane ticket and other transportation costs to Nigeria, I will pay you back $250,000 from my fist payment.  
My recent versions usually site an inheritance that you need to pay a lawyer to verify you are the lost relative. They all promise a large payout if only you first pay a smaller amount for something.  
There was a theory that these were poorly written on purpose. So that if a person responded, they were extremely dim witted and not wasting the scammers time on a smart person that would never go for sending the money.
