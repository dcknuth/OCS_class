## Scam Time: Ransomware
This attack encrypts your data and then tries to sell you the key to decrypt
* Tells you might be infected
    - Performance issues
    - Renamed files
    - Odd content opening a file
* Reinstall right away
* I was affected many years back
    - Probably cross-site scripting from yahoo.com
    - Good backups saved me (and some luck)
* Another version of this is to publicly release stolen data
